Community Vision

Rumi’s legacy is a unique and timeless treasure

NoorAva is a space dedicated to the multidimensional exploration of Rumi’s work coupled with Hekmat-e Khosrovani — offering a holistic experience through reading, interpretation, music, and dance. Our aim is to illuminate the layered depth of his teachings by presenting them in an integrated, immersive manner that engages both intellect and spirit. True understanding requires a multidimensional and holistic approach — one that transcends isolated interpretations and embraces the interconnectedness of thought, sound, movement, and meaning. NoorAva endeavors to draw upon Iranian cultural and national heritage to help shape a cultivated and conscious national identity. We create spaces for reflection, dialogue, and collective renewal.

A multidimensional path of understanding

NoorAva believes true understanding requires a holistic approach that embraces the interconnectedness of thought, sound, movement, and meaning.

Holistic

Reading

The first step in understanding Rumi’s work is the ability to read the poems correctly. Poetry asks to be entered slowly, allowing each word, pause, image, and cadence to breathe.

Contemplative

Interpretation & Analysis

Interpretation reveals the soul of the poem; analysis reveals its architecture. With Rumi, both become doorways into transformation rather than conclusions to possess.

Immersive

Music

Music harmonizes the soul with nature and the whole of existence. NoorAva presents forms of music aligned with Persian literature, mystical tradition, peace, and joy.

Embodied

Dance

When beauty and love are poured into our being, they become poetry, music, dance, or all three together. Movement becomes another path back to the source from which art arises.
