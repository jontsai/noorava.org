About the Event

The boundless nature of the human spirit

The Boundless Concert takes its name from the boundless nature of the human spirit — the limitless depth of music, poetry, and wisdom to awaken, inspire, and connect us beyond language, culture, and time. Interwoven throughout the evening will be brief presentations introducing NoorAva, a nonprofit cultural and arts foundation dedicated to preserving and sharing the timeless wisdom of Rumi and the Philosophy of Light.

The first set explores the ancient modal traditions of Persia, drawing from a musical lineage preserved for thousands of years. The second set presents contemporary Persian classical compositions alongside timeless songs of love, longing, and devotion.

Performers

platforms

Alireza Shahmohammadi

Composer, singer, classic Persian music

Alireza Shahmohammadi is an Iranian composer, vocalist, educator, and Rumi scholar whose work is deeply grounded in the Persian classical tradition.

Pejman Hadadi

Percussionist

Pejman Hadadi is a Tehran-born Iranian percussionist renowned for innovative work in Persian rhythm, signature techniques, teaching, and cross-cultural collaborations.

Behfar Bahadoran

Tar, Setar, multi-instrumentalist

Behfar Bahadoran is a multi-instrumentalist and interdisciplinary artist rooted in Persian classical, regional, and Kurdish mystic traditions.
